## Getting Started

To get started with our product, sign up at our website and follow the
onboarding wizard. You'll need to provide your email address and create
a password.

## Billing

We offer three plans: Free, Pro ($10/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing).
All plans include a 14-day free trial. You can cancel anytime from your
account settings page.

To upgrade or downgrade your plan, go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan.
Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Common Issues

### I forgot my password

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your
registered email address. The link expires after 1 hour.

### My account is locked

Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes and try
again, or contact support for immediate unlock.

### I'm not receiving emails

Check your spam/junk folder first. Add noreply@example.com to your contacts.
If emails still don't arrive, verify your email address in Settings > Profile.
